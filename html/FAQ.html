How to distinguish between towers from the front and back side of Acad Block?

It's easy to navigate towers from outside of Acad Block, if you are at flagpole side, then T1 starts from Main entry gate side and T4 is at opposite end of the building, with T2 and T3 lying in between , if you are at feild side, then T1 starts from Cricket feild side and T4 can be locatted at the other end of the building, with T2 and T3 lying in between.

How and where to give stuffs for laundry ? (Sticker price+reg.)

Once you go to the laundry desk, they'll give u a laundry bag. U have 2options then, either to get stickers or they'll write your laundry number with a marker. 500rs for 100 stickers. It's advisable not to take stickers as they are expensive. Go for markers as their marks won't be shown on your clothes.
